Viscount Mining and Lux Partners Ltd. create the world's first silver-backed NFT. 
Jim MacKenzie, CEO of Viscount Mining stated, "We are thrilled to partner with Lux and provide 
them with access to an initial 5 million ounces of silver from our property in Silver Cliff, Colorado. 
Our team has worked tirelessly to bring this project to fruition, and we are confident 
that our silver will be an excellent addition to the Lux ecosystem and to global markets".
